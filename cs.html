Pizza Store Sales Analysis

PROBLEM STATEMENT

A pizza store wants to understand what's happening with the business in order to plan for the next year. They are seeking reports to track and assess the progress.

TASKS

  • Performed data cleaning and analysed customer service metrics, overall sales, and revenue.
  • Created dynamic slicers allowing users to interact with the dashboards.
  • Utilized pivot tables to address queries such as the daily average sales, top-selling products, and average customer satisfaction rate.

EXCEL DASHBOARDS

Created interactive dashboards for Customer service, finance and orders.

KEY INSIGHTS

  • Analysis suggested ‘Panner Tikka Pizza’ is the best-selling product, contributing the highest revenue.
  • Agent Adrien Martin has the highest customer satisfaction rate.
  • Product 5 and 6 are not performing well and needs a different strategy.
  • 70% of all the sales are happening between the ticket sizes of 500 and 900.
  • The decline in sales to an average of 5000 started by the End of July.
